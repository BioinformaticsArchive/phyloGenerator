Make realistic phylogenies quickly and easily

...and be confident that they will not conflict with existing taxonomic opinions

  • Overview

    Making phylogenies is hard enough for phylogeneticists, but it's something that ecologists are forced to do more and more frequently - especially in the field of community phylogenetics. I think it's a shame that lots of effort goes into generating ecological data, while the (equally important!) phylogeny is often neglected.

  • Branch lengths matter

    With phyloGenerator, you can download real DNA sequence data for your species of interest, and then generate a calibrated phylogeny using a defensible constraint tree. Your phylogeny may not be 'correct', but it will have accurate branch lengths and its topology will be safe. In other words, it's be a 'real' phylogeny, not a best-guess from taxonomy filled with polytomies.

  • A work in progress...

    Please, feel free to use the code, and any phylogenies it generates. However, you are not permitted to publish anything derrived from this software, or to use the code in any project of your own, until I've published this program. I'm planning to submit a paper to Methods in Ecology and Evolution within a week, so don't worry though!
